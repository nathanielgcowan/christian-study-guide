## Introduction

One day Jesus’ disciples asked Him, “Lord, teach us to pray.” Jesus gave us this beautiful prayer as a model.

## The Scripture Passage

**Matthew 6:9-13 (ESV)**

Our Father in heaven,  
hallowed be your name.  
Your kingdom come,  
your will be done,  
on earth as it is in heaven.  
Give us this day our daily bread,  
and forgive us our debts,  
as we also have forgiven our debtors.  
And lead us not into temptation,  
but deliver us from evil.

## Breaking It Down

### 1. “Our Father in heaven”

Jesus invites us to call the holy God of the universe **“Father”** — this shows love and respect.

### 2. “Hallowed be your name”

We start by honoring God’s name.
